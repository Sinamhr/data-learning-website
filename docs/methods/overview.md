- [Physics-informed and hybrid models](./physics-informed)
- [Sequence and dynamics-based models](./sequence-dynamics)
- [Graph-based methods, geometric models and unstructured meshes](./graph-geometric)
- [Models for explainability and uncertainty](./explainability-uncertainty)
- [Generative models and synthetic augmentation](./generative-synthetic)
- [Reinforcement learning](./reinforcement-learning)
